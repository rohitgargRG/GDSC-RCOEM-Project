FAQs

What is GDSC?

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are
community groups for college and university students
interested in Google developer technologies. Students
from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an
interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By
joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a
peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions
for local businesses and their community.

How can you become a part of GDSC ?

We conduct an annual team recruitment process. The
details of recruitment are posted online a few weeks
prior. It's a two step process involving form filling and
personal interview. Students acing both rounds are
selected to be the part of the core team.

What does a GDSC Lead do?

A GDSC Lead fills a lot of shoes. He works with the
university to build the club, recruit the core team, host
workshops, build projects, collaborate with local
partners.
